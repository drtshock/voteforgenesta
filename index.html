WHY I'M RUNNING

I'm running for Student Senate President because I believe every student deserves meaningful representation in the decisions that shape our academic experience.

Right now, too many students feel disconnected from campus governance. That needs to change. I want to bridge that gap through transparent communication, proactive advocacy, and leadership that actually includes you.

Effective student government demands active listening, strategic planning, and genuine commitment to our community. I'm ready to show up and do the work.
