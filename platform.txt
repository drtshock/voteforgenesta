Building a Stronger Student Voice at Saint Paul College

I’m running for Student Senate President because I believe every student deserves meaningful representation in the decisions that shape our academic experience. Currently, many students feel disconnected from campus governance. My goal is to bridge that gap through transparent communication, proactive advocacy, and inclusive leadership. 

My platform centers on three core commitments:

Affordable and Equitable Education. I will advocate strongly during tuition and fee consultations to ensure student financial concerns are prioritized. Higher education should be attainable for all students, regardless of economic background.
Transparent and Responsive Governance. I will modernize our outreach efforts through diverse communication channels—social media, campus events, and direct forums—to ensure Senate is responsive and accessible to the entire student body.
Inclusive Leadership and Opportunity. I will strengthen support for student organizations, expand leadership development opportunities, and ensure underrepresented voices are actively included in campus decision-making processes.

Effective student government demands active listening, strategic planning, and genuine commitment to serving our community. I am prepared to fulfill these responsibilities with professionalism and dedication.

Together, we can build a Student Senate that truly represents and advocates for all Saint Paul College students.

Genesta Cordero for Student Senate President
